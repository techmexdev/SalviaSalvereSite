Salvia Salvere

About

Salvia Salvere is an Indie-Folk band from Houston, Texas. The current lineup consists of Danh Bui on ukulele and lead vocals, Kesley Richardson on Guitar, Bass, mandolin and backing vocals, and Kevin Romero on drums. As of now, Saliva has three EP releases, two of which were written, recorded and preformed exclusively by Danh Bui.

Salvia started as a solo project by Danh while he was in another band, Townsage, with Kesley Richardson. After the first few releases, Kesley asked Danh if he could contribute to the next set of recordings and thus Cabin Fever was born over a weekend. Kevin Romero came slightly afterwords in early 2017 after he and Danh met through a mutual friend.

The band is currently working on a full length album set to be released sometime in 2017 as well a 7 song EP containing re-recordings of many of the band's pre-kevin songs.

The name Salvia Salvere was derived from Kesley and Danh's former band's name, Townsage. Salvia is the Latin name for the plant sage, and Salvere means to save; literally translating to, Sage Saves.
